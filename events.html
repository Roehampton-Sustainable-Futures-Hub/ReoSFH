Academic Collaboration Showcase

Every collaboration begins with a conversation, and the Academic Collaboration Showcase was created to spark exactly that, bringing together businesses, community partners and researchers at the University of Roehampton.

Across the building, research teams share the work they are developing: sustainable engineering projects designed to reduce environmental impact; digital and AI innovations that help organisations work smarter; health and wellbeing research that supports communities; creative technologies that open new possibilities for storytelling and design.

For businesses and partners, the showcase is an opportunity to discover what becomes possible when industry challenges meet research insight. Conversations unfold around resilience, sustainability, digital transformation and community impact.

The event also highlights the university's commitment to community‑led work, including recent engagement with local business ecosystems. It reflects a simple belief: that innovation grows stronger when it is shared.

The showcase is more than an exhibition. It is an open invitation to connect, explore and imagine what we can build together.
