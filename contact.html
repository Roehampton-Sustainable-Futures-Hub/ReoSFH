Work With Us

The Roehampton Sustainable Futures Hub is open to new partnerships, research collaborations, community-led project ideas and funding conversations. We work with organisations and individuals across London who share our commitment to people, planet and partnership.

Location: University of Roehampton, London, United Kingdom
Email: sustainabilitycommunityproject@gmail.com

We work alongside the London Borough of Kingston upon Thames, the London Borough of Wandsworth, the London Borough of Hammersmith & Fulham, A Greener London, The Community Brain, Kingston Hive, Sustainable Merton, Putney Action Group and London-based SMEs.
